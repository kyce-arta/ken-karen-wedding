The Flow

Evening timeline

We’ve planned a relaxed, joy‑filled celebration. Arrive a little early, stay as late as you like.

2:00 PM
Wedding ceremony
A short, heartfelt ceremony as we exchange vows surrounded by the people we love most.
4:00 PM
Guest arrival & Registration
Doors open and light refreshments will be served as you mingle and find your seat for the ceremony.
4:15 PM
Pica-Pica (Finger Food)
Drinks, hors d’oeuvres, and city views while we take photos with family and friends.
5:30 PM
Grand Entrance
The celebration officially begins as the entourage enters the reception. Sponsors, bridesmaids, groomsmen, and finally the newly married couple make their grand entrance to welcome cheers and applause.
6:00 PM
Dinner & Games
A seated dinner followed by toasts, cake, and games.
8:30 PM
Closing
The night ends with a sparkler send-off, group photo, or final song to celebrate the beginning of their new life together.
8:40 PM
After party + Open Bar
The dance floor opens for everyone. Guests are invited to celebrate, drink and dance, and enjoy the rest of the evening with the couple.
